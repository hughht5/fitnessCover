Fitness Cover FAQ

Why should i use fitness cover?

1. Fitness cover avoids the hassle of the instructor or fitness manager having to find cover for their class or someone else's.

2. Cover instructors get instant alerts if a class is available for cover and all the alerts will be relevant to you so you wont get alerts for covers which you would not be qualified to teach or that are not in your area.

3. Our quick pay system means that you don't have to wait weeks to get paid (i know from experience that it can be months!) you will be paid for the class within 3 days.

4. Class instructors that work in a specific Gym will get priority to cover the classes in that same club.

5. Fitness Cover will work on a first come first serve basis so fastest finger first!

6. If you are a reliable instructor and get rave reviews from clients you may be prioritised to cover a class.

How do i fill in the invoice with fitness cover details?

When you turn up to the gym make sure you sign in as normal so the manager knows that you have covered the class. FitnessCover will then contact the manager to ensure everything went smoothly and we will pay you within 3 working days.

Fitness cover will then invoice the clubs directly.

How can i guarantee that Fitness Cover will pay me?

As a fitness instructor myself I completely understand that you are trusting me to pay you within a certain time frame. At Fitness Cover we guarantee that we will pay you within this time. Simply put, our reputation depends on it.

Why do i need to give my bank details?

So i can pay you for your good work! All of your information is confidential so you don't need to worry. Also, the information you give us allows us to pay money in, but not take money out.

Why are there only certain classes that are available to cover?

These classes have been selected because they are the most popular classes in corporate gyms. They appear on the timetables more than any other classes so there will be a greater chance of them needing cover. If you have suggestions for more classes please contact us.

I don't have a ppl license because Gym X don't require it...?

To teach at Fitness First clubs you don't need a PPL license but Fitness Cover require this to ensure that no issues occur with our cover instructors in the future when we expand to arrange cover for other clubs which do require it.
