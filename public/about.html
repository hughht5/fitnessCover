About Us

Fitness Cover

Organising Cover

At Fitness Cover we provide cover instructors for your classes when you can't make it, taking away the hassle of organising it yourself.

Quickpay

For cover instructors we contact you when relevant classes become available in your area and through our quickpay system we guarantee to pay you within 3 working days.

Annie Morgan, Founder

As some of you may know I am a personal trainer and fitness instructor in London. I teach spin, circuits, boxing, easytone step and reformer Pilates at a range of gyms but predominantly Fitness First!

Since January I have been covering predominantly spin classes and now teach 16 permanent classes a week (spin, circuits and Pilates) along with extras covers wherever I can fit them in.

When I started work I was in some serious debt from my MSc in forensic psychology that I had completed 6 months earlier. I soon realised that the gyms were unreliable with their payments and I am currently still waiting for pay from January. This made the last 6 months really hard work and it became hard to motivate myself when I wasn't getting paid on time for any of the work I was doing.

I also know the problems that managers have trying to get cover with poor signal in gyms and lots of time wasted trying to do a ring around.

After realising that it wasn't just me and other instructors were effected by inconsistent pay, Fitness Cover was invented.

Hugh Halford-Thompson, Founder

I am a software architect and entrepreneur. I like to use technology to fix issues in a range of industries. When Annie told me of the inefficiencies in covering fitness classes and specifically chasing payments I decided something should be done.

Working in software I enjoy the benefits of automation every day. I built the website for FitnessCover and the processes it uses to enable fitness instructors to spend less of their free time organising cover and chasing payments. Through FitnessCover I strive to enable fitness instructors to enjoy more freedom.
